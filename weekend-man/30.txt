THE RULES

ACTIONS

<h3>
ACTION TYPES
</h3>

During their activation, Warriors may carry out actions. The number of actions is
limited to the number of the Action Characteristic. The armour choice decides this and
will be 3, 4 or 5.

Actions may be carried out in any order and any number of times as long as
the Warrior has enough Actions. 

<POSTIT>
For example, a Warrior with 3 Actions can:
Move - Move - Move
Attack - Attack - Attack
Move - Attack - Move
Move - Attack - Push - Move 
</POSTIT>

The Actions a Warrior can carry out are as follows:
